Example Case 3 - Year Planning

Andrew Rosemberg
15th Feb 2019

Introduction

This an example of the HydroPowerModels package for solving a simple stochastic case with the following specifications:

  • 3 Buses

  • 3 Lines

  • 2 Generators

  • 1 Reservoir and Hydrogenerator

  • 3 Scenarios

  • 12 Stages
